**Rack Myself** is a homelab and self-hosting guide site. The goal is simple: write the guides that should exist — practical, accurate, updated with current hardware and software, not padded with filler.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Home server hardware** — mini PCs, NAS drives, networking gear, real power consumption numbers
- **Self-hosting guides** — Docker, Nextcloud, Jellyfin, password managers, reverse proxies
- **Local AI** — running Ollama, Open WebUI, and current LLMs on consumer hardware
- **Networking** — Cloudflare Tunnels, OpenWrt, VLANs, pfSense

## How This Site Works

Guides are written and kept current. When hardware prices change or software updates break old instructions, articles get updated. If something is wrong or out of date, the intent is to fix it — not leave stale content sitting around.

Amazon links are affiliate links. If you buy something through a link here, this site earns a small commission at no extra cost to you. It's how the site stays running.
